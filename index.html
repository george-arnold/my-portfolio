I'm George, a Full Stack Web Developer from Annapolis, MD.

What is my coding journey?

As a teacher, I quickly transitioned to teaching tech and computer science. I fell in love with coding because I have a knack for creative thinking, and I love solving problems. After discovering my love of code, I taught myself for a year. Looking to accelerate the self-teaching process, I enrolled in a web developer bootcamp, which provided me with a mentor, and the tools to get my skills up to date.

Why do you want me on your team?

I have over a decade of work experience, and I'm a self-taught, highly driven developer. I am always looking to learn new things, and I have acquired a myriad of skills beyond code that provide value in additon to my value as a developer.
